Waffles
From Cooks Illustrated cookbook

Ingredients
    1 c. flour
    1/2 t. salt
    1/4 t. baking soda
    1/2 t. cream of tartar
    1 egg, separated
    3/4 c. milk
    2 T. butter, melted, cooled

Directions
    1. Heat waffle iron
    2. whisk dry ingredients in m. bowl
    3. whisk egg yolk with butter, then milk, in a 2-cup pyrex 
    4. beat egg white until 2-in. peak
    5. fold liquid into dry with rubber spatula
    6. fold in egg white
    7. cook

Serve

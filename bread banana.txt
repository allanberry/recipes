Banana Bread
adapted from Cook's Illustrated cookbook

Ingredients
    3 c. flour, with a little more for the pans
    2 c. walnuts, chopped coarse, toasted
    1 1/2 c. sugar
    1 1/4 t. baking soda
    3/4 t. salt
    5 lg./6 sm. old brown bananas, mashed
    1/2 c. yogurt
    3 eggs, beaten
    10 T. (1 1/4 sticks) butter, melted, cooled
    1 1/2 t. vanilla

Directions
    1. preheat oven to 350.
    2. grease bottom and sides of two 4x8 in. bread tins; shake some flour inside to coat.  Tap out excess flour.
    4. whisk flour, sugar, soda, salt, walnuts in lg. bowl
    5. mix bananas, yogurt, eggs, butter, vanilla in md. bowl
    6. fold moist ingredients into dry ingredients, until just combined.  Thick and chunky.
    7. scrape batter into pans
    8. bake until a toothpick, placed in center, comes out clean; ~55 mins.  Transfer to wire rack.

Serve warm.
